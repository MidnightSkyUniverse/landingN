About Me

My Philosophy

"I don't build to serve or to help anyone. I don't build to have clients. I have clients to build."

Howard Roark, The Fountainhead

This quote captures my approach perfectly. My purpose is to create the most valuable, best-constructed, and most impactful training and solutions possible. I strive to offer the best I have and teach others to recognize excellence in AI implementation.

My Story

After 20 years navigating between corporate environments and running my own company, I've witnessed firsthand the transformation that technology brings to business. But I've also seen the fear and confusion that often accompanies it.

Many professionals today fall into two camps: those who are scared of AI and avoid it entirely, or those who blindly copy solutions without understanding why they work. Neither approach leads to sustainable success.

That's why I don't just teach AI tools—I guide people on how to think about AI and how to best work alongside it. My mission is simple: turning "I don't know if this is for me" into "I know exactly how to use this."
